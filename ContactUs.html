Contact Us - Emergency Helplines

If you're stuck in a bad weather situation or any other emergency, here are some important helplines that can help you.

Emergency Contact Numbers

  • Emergency Helpline (Police, Fire, Ambulance):

    This number connects you to the Police, Fire Services, Ambulance, and Disaster Management Teams.

    112
  • National Disaster Management (NDMA):

    For guidance and help in case of natural disasters like floods, cyclones, and storms.

    1078
  • Ambulance & Medical Emergency:

    Dial this number for medical emergencies, including accidents or health issues.

    108
  • Indian Meteorological Department (IMD):

    For weather updates, warnings, and alerts during severe weather conditions like cyclones and heavy rainfall.

    +91 11 24623535
  • Roadside Assistance (IRDA):

    If your vehicle breaks down or you are stuck due to bad weather, call for roadside help.

    1033
